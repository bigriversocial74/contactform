# Stage 5F Merchant Intelligence, Campaign Analytics, Demand Signals, and Operational Reporting

Stage 5F integrates the Stage 4F intelligence foundation into the shared merchant workspace.

## Included

- Date-range merchant analytics
- Demand, engagement, claim, redemption, and issued-value KPIs
- PPPM lifecycle funnel
- Daily demand trend
- Product, campaign, and location comparisons
- Merchant intelligence scores and insights
- Demand forecasts and confidence ranges
- Open alert visibility
- Privacy-safe export queue integration
- Saved report definitions and hashed-recipient reporting schedules

## Boundaries

Forecasts are decision-support signals, not guaranteed revenue. Customer-level exports are not introduced. Payment processing and financial accounting remain separate stages.
