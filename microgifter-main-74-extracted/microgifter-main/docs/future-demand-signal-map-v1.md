# Future Demand Signal Map v1

## Purpose

This document maps the value-added signals that can later feed Future Demand Profiles, demand readiness, demand velocity, regional demand, category demand, and enterprise local impact models.

Stage 6C records the map only. It does not implement scoring.

## Signal families

### Historical behavior

Signals that show what has already happened:

- purchases
- completed orders
- repeat buyers
- claimed gifts
- redeemed items
- fulfilled orders
- reviews
- content engagement
- customer messages
- claim completion rate
- redemption completion rate
- refund or dispute history

### Demand-ready assets

Signals that show a profile can capture future demand:

- published products
- digital products
- giftable offers
- claimable assets
- active store
- active locations
- available services
- event listings
- content library
- downloads
- subscriptions
- interaction options

### Trust and quality

Signals that affect perceived demand capacity:

- reviews
- rating quality
- verified profile
- verified location
- claim success rate
- fulfillment reliability
- response rate
- account age
- active status
- moderation state

### Reach and context

Signals that define where and how demand may occur:

- city
- region
- ZIP code
- neighborhood
- category
- profile type
- location count
- service area
- employee range
- audience size
- enterprise program participation

### Future opportunity

Signals that indicate demand that may be activated later:

- scheduled gifts
- recurring rewards
- saved products
- wishlisted products
- enterprise commitments
- workplace reward allocations
- customer incentive programs
- future visits
- campaign reservations
- unredeemed committed demand

## Initial profile metrics

Early profile pages can safely show simple non-predictive counts:

- products
- assets
- locations
- reviews
- orders
- claims
- redemptions
- interaction options
- active programs
- committed demand amount

## Later intelligence metrics

Stage 15 and Stage 16 can introduce:

- Demand Readiness Score
- Future Demand Score
- Committed Demand
- Future Demand Pipeline
- Demand Velocity
- Regional Demand Index
- Category Demand Index
- Enterprise Local Impact Score
- Projected Local Demand Activity

## Interpretation guardrail

The platform should present these metrics as platform signals and projections, not guarantees. The correct claim is that the signals indicate demand readiness and opportunity density based on recorded behavior and available demand pathways.
